# 📄 PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

# Integrated Farm Management System (IFMS)

### Version 1.0 — “Farm Brain Core”

---

# 1. PRODUCT VISION

To build a **real-time, intelligent livestock operating system** that unifies dairy production, goat & sheep genetics, hatchery operations, and poultry production into a single data-driven ecosystem that:

* Captures all farm activities in real time
* Converts raw farm activity into financial intelligence
* Optimizes profitability per animal, batch, and enterprise
* Automates decision-making through rules and alerts
* Enables scalable, transparent farm management

---

# 2. PROBLEM STATEMENT

Most livestock farms in West Africa suffer from:

* Fragmented record keeping (paper, WhatsApp, memory)
* No real-time visibility of production or health status
* Lack of cost-per-unit understanding (milk, meat, chick)
* Weak reproductive tracking → long calving intervals
* Poor batch performance tracking in poultry
* No integration between enterprises
* Delayed or missing financial reporting
* Reactive rather than predictive management

---

# 3. PRODUCT GOALS

## Primary Goals

1. Centralize all farm data into one system
2. Enable real-time monitoring of farm operations
3. Link every animal/batch to financial performance
4. Improve reproductive efficiency across species
5. Reduce operational waste and inefficiency
6. Enable data-driven decision-making

---

## Secondary Goals

* Enable mobile-first farm operations
* Provide WhatsApp-based reporting
* Support offline data capture
* Enable future AI-based predictions
* Support scalability to multi-farm operations
